Information that Fifa Investment Fund automatically collects

When you use our website, we may collect the Internet Protocol (IP) address of your computer and your Internet service provider, the date and time you access our website, the Internet address of the website from which that linked directly to our website, the computer operating system you are using, the sections of our website you visit, the website pages read and images viewed, and the content you download from the website. This information is used for website and system administration purposes and to improve the website.

Cookies – Fifa Investment Fund may use cookies to store your individual preferences and system information. "Cookies" are small electronic data files that websites may store on your computer for record-keeping purposes.

For example, cookies will remember what you have placed in your shopping cart and tell us that you have visited our website before. Cookies may also be used on the website for other reasons, such as to limit the number of times you must enter a password or enter your language preference, to offer personalized web page content and information for your use, and to track your activity on the website.

The use of cookies is standard in the Internet industry and many websites use them. In addition, you can modify your browser settings to notify you each time a website is about to deposit a cookie on your computer's hard drive or other storage media.

The notification option generally gives you the opportunity to decide whether to accept or reject a cookie on a case-by-case basis. You can set your browser to always disable cookies when cookies are sent to your computer, although this may affect your ability to shop online, both on the Fifa Investment Fund and elsewhere.

Website Tracking Information: Web beacons are small graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of web users. We may use web beacons (sometimes called "clear gifs," "web bugs," or "pixel tags") to compile reports of tracking information about website user demographics, website traffic patterns, and purchases. on the website.

We can then provide these reports to advertisers and others. None of the tracking information in these reports can be connected to the identities or other personal information of individual users. Additionally, we may use web beacons in our HTML-based emails to let us know which emails have been opened by recipients. This allows us to measure the effectiveness of certain communications and the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

If you do not wish to receive these emails, please see the "unsubscribe" section below. For Fifa Investment Fund's own research purposes, we may link tracking information to personal information voluntarily provided by website users. Once such a link is made, all linked information is treated as personal information and will be used and disclosed only in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

How does Fifa Investment Fund protects your information?

The file containing your personal information will be stored on our servers and will only be accessible to our employees and those of our agents and service providers who require it in the course of their duties. Fifa Investment Fund takes appropriate measures to protect your personal information. We maintain appropriate safeguards and current security standards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

For example, electronic records are stored on secure servers with limited access; electronic data is stored behind secure encryption access; we use technological tools such as firewalls and passwords; and we ensure that our employees are trained on the importance of keeping personal information secure and confidential. SSL Technology: Our website uses encryption technology, such as Secure Sockets Layer ("SSL"), to protect your personal information during data transport. SSL encrypts order information such as your name, address, and credit card number.

Choose a password: When you register as a user and create an online account on our website, you will be asked to select a personal password. To maximize your level of protection, you should choose a strong personal password,

which means you must use at least 6 characters, including a combination of letters and numbers. You are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your password and any account information. Fifa Investment Fund will never send an unsolicited communication asking you for your password or requesting that you update your account credentials.

Although we cannot assume responsibility for any theft, misuse, unauthorized disclosure, loss, alteration, or destruction of data by a third party, we take reasonable precautions to prevent such occurrences.
